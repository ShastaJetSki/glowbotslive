02 Scale & clear space

The mark reads cleanly from 16px favicons up to oversize signage. Maintain at least one half-bar of clear space around every instance.

Clear space rule
Keep a minimum padding equal to half the height of one bar on all sides of the mark. When in doubt, give it more air. The mark is confident enough to hold its own space.

05 Do & don't

Protect the mark. A few simple rules keep the brand consistent across every surface.
✓
Do use the gradient tilefor primary placements — app icons, headers, favicons.
✓
Do use solid colorwhen the mark appears on colored surfaces or in single-ink print.
✓
Do maintain clear spaceequal to half a bar height around the mark.
✓
Do pair "Solutions" in italic cyanwhen using the full wordmark in editorial contexts.
✕
Don't stretch or skewthe proportions of the mark — always scale uniformly.
✕
Don't recolor the barswith the brand gradient. The bars are always a solid color.
✕
Don't add effects— no drop shadows on the bars, no outer glows on the mark itself.
✕
Don't place on busy photographywithout a solid tile or scrim behind it.
